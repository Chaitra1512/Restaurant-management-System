Restaurant Reservation and billing system:

The Restaurent Reservation and Billing system is a user friendly system designed to enhance the customer experience which involves different features like:

-> Table reservation: To reserve the table of customer's choice from the list of tables available where the list is updated after every reservation.

-> Food ordering: The food menu is displayed alongwith the price to the customer where he/she can order the food from the menu.

-> Bill generation: The bill is generated by calculating the prices of the food ordered and the bill is generated.

-> Payment: Finally the payment is processed where the customer can pay the bills. Overall, a Restaurant Billing and Reservation System enhances operational efficiency, improves customer satisfaction, and drives business growth for restaurant owners/managers, while providing convenience to the customers.
